HOW TO OFFER A RIDE?

CREATE A RIDE

Enter your start and end locations, select the route, enter the start time, cost per seat and done!

GET REQUESTS

See requests from potential Riders, view their profiles and pickup/drop points and make them a part of your ride!

RIDE ALONG

Begin your ride, pickup Riders and help them reach their destinations. Rate and review your Riders and get paid. Could it be easier?

HOW TO TAKE A RIDE?

SEARCH FOR RIDES

Enter your start and end locations, start time and distance flexibility and view the potential rides on a map with pricings.

SEND REQUESTS

View driver and the route details and send requests for the rides you feel will be best for you. Once the Offeror accepts your request, sit back and relax!

RIDE ALONG

Get notified when the ride is started, get picked up and reach your destination like a boss!
